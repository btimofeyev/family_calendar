Safety Standards

At FamlyNook, we prioritize the safety and security of all family members using our platform. This document outlines our standards and practices designed to maintain a safe environment, particularly for children.

Our Approach to Safety

FamlyNook is designed as a private platform exclusively for families. All content is only visible to approved family members, providing an inherently protected environment. Unlike public social networks, our closed-group model significantly reduces safety risks.

Content Standards

We maintain a strict policy against any harmful or exploitative content, particularly content that could harm children. Our platform is designed for sharing family memories, events, and communications in a positive, supportive environment.

The following content is strictly prohibited:

  • Any content that depicts or promotes child exploitation
  • Content that threatens, harasses, or bullies any family member
  • Content that infringes on privacy or could lead to identity theft
  • Content that violates applicable laws or regulations

Safety Features

FamlyNook incorporates several features to maintain safety:

  • Closed family groups with invitation-only access
  • Content visibility limited to approved family members only
  • Ability to report inappropriate content within the app
  • Prompt review of all reported content

Reporting Concerns

If you encounter content that violates our safety standards, please use the in-app reporting feature or contact us directly. We review all reports promptly and take appropriate action.
