FamlyNook Account Deletion

Account Deletion Policy

At FamlyNook, we respect your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time through our mobile application or by contacting our support team.

How to Delete Your Account
  1. Open the FamlyNook app on your device
  2. Go to your Profile tab
  3. Scroll down to "Account Settings"
  4. Tap on "Delete My Account"
  5. Follow the confirmation steps
What Happens When You Delete Your Account

When you request account deletion:

  • We will send you an email to confirm your request
  • Once confirmed, your account and personal data will be permanently deleted within 30 days
  • This includes your profile information, posts, comments, photos, and family connections
  • Some data may remain in our backup systems for up to 90 days
  • Content you shared with other users may remain visible even after your account is deleted

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

Alternative Methods to Request Deletion

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by:

  • Emailing us at support@famlynook.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  • Providing your registered email address and full name in the email
Contact Information
Email: support@famlynook.com

Data Retention Policy

We retain user data only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. When you delete your account:

  • Your personal information will be deleted from our active systems
  • Some information may be retained in backup systems for up to 90 days
  • We may retain certain information if necessary to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or enforce our agreements

For more detailed information about how we handle your data, please refer to our Privacy Policy.
